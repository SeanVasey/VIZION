00

Brand essence

An instrument console, not a chat box. Precise, technical, and quietly confident — the discipline of a studio rack unit with a single live signal light. The palette is monochrome restraint punctuated by one electric green; the personality lives in the restraint.

01

Logo & wordmark

VIZ(IO)N reads as vision; structurally it is viz · io · n. The parentheses are a lens aperture; the enclosed IO is the focal point where Input becomes Output.

VIZ(IO)N
Chalk letterforms · Silver aperture brackets at ~40% weight · Laser IO, one weight heavier · optional hairline caret I › O at large sizes only
(I›O)
App-icon glyph — Void tile · transparent-PNG suite · manifest declares any + maskable
DoKeep IO in Laser; everything around it quiet. Use the aperture as the only ornament.
Don'tSet IO in Laser as text on a light background, recolor the brackets, or add a second accent hue.

05

Spacing & elevation

4px base unit on an 8-point rhythm. Elevation is built from translucency and light — frosted glass and edge-glow — not drop shadow.

Base
Void canvas. The ground plane — no elevation.
Raised
Onyx glass · blur 16px · 1px Silver hairline at 20%.
Focused
Glass + Laser edge-glow. Reserved for active / focus.
Iconography: 1.5px stroke · rounded joins · 24px grid · Silver at rest, Laser when active. Provider & dev logos sourced from thesvg.org, optimized via Potrace/SVGO.
06

The signature

The transformation diff is the brand's one memorable gesture: input on the Void side, enhanced output on the lighter side, changed tokens lit in Laser, with a plain-language rationale.
